Inspire students to

It is important to us that every student we come into contact with feels inspired to help others, has the strength to get through another day, and wants to be the change we all want to see in the world.

Day 1 (Tuesday)

All School Assembly

We launch our program with an a powerful, inspirational, and motivational all school assembly. During the assembly, we discus the hard truth regarding mental health.

It is important that all students can hear and see the the struggle that others may be facing on a daily basis. The purpose of this assembly is to open the eyes of each student to the world of mental health, inspire them to be the change, and encourage them to take action.

Days 2 - 7 (Wednesday - Wednesday)

Action Days

Inspire

For the next 6 days, students will watch inspiring stories of struggling individuals and how a little positivity can affect their life.

Students will watch a 10 minute "Walk-up episode" each morning that will educate them on mental health, normalize the conversation, and inspire them to be the love, support, and help struggling individuals may need!

Encourage

The purpose of each episode is for the students to understand that everyone has a story.

Most importantly, we want the students to feel encouraged and feel the sense of responsibility to be the person that can change someone’s life.

Day 8 (Thursday)

Round Up

It is time to gather as a school again and talk about the what we have learned. This is the point in the program where we hope to have students come up and share their stories. When students see people going up and sharing their story without judgement, they will feel encouraged to talk and ask for help if needed.

This is also they day where the students begin their task of raising money to help bring the help and support onto campus that can change the direction of a student’s life. They will have 36 hours to go out and share the schools “walkupevent” page with their supporters.
